What do I learn from these courses?

The entire course consists of 3 parts
1
Introduction

In this section, we will get acquainted with the history of man, who retrained as a specialist in artificial intelligence and answer the question: “Is it worth it takes 2 years to study artificial intelligence?"

2
Introduction to programming.

This section begins by explaining the concepts of problems, the essence, and the goals of artificial intelligence. You will learn the difference between data and information, as well as what information quality means. We will discuss what algorithms, computer programs, and the Python programming language are, and start working in an integrated development environment for Python. Next, we will understand the nature of variables, their properties, and types. It is from this section that you will start writing your first code in the Python programming language. We will study all aspects of this remarkable language. Following that, we will explore libraries such as NumPy, Pandas, and Matplotlib. In this section, you will get acquainted with the Jupyter Notebook development environment.

3
Introduction to artificial intelligence.

This section shows us the difference between linear and nonlinear programming. Describes the process of obtaining knowledge. Also, you will find out what is the difference between AI, machine learning and deep learning. We will also discuss the types artificial intelligence, its applications and examples.

4
Machine learning algorithm.

In these sections, we will analyze in detail how the algorithms work. machine learning. And you will create a very simple one, but your own artificial intelligence model. Also you will learn evaluate the accuracy of models. In this section we detail let's start analyzing one of the libraries of artificial intelligence scikit learn. Here we will analyze another environment development called spyder.

5
The process of developing an artificial intelligence model and data engineering.

Here we will discuss in detail the entire process of model development artificial intelligence. We will also begin studying data analysis and processing for artificial training intelligence. In this section I will teach you practical examples of data analysis and processing from A to Z.

6
Feature selection.

In this section, from all the data we have, we will learn select only those that we need for training artificial intelligence.

7
Checklist for almost all man-made projects intelligence.

In this section I will give a checklist of actions for building end-to-end artificial intelligence projects.

8
Practical lessons.

In practical exercises, we will teach our artificial intelligence models the following tasks: predicting user clicks on Facebook ads; diagnosing breast cancer; predicting bank customer attrition; detecting kyphosis in children; determining positive and negative Yelp reviews; identifying positive and negative reviews on Amazon; detecting spam emails; predicting the presence of heart disease in patients; and identifying wear and tear on parts of a lathe.

9
Deep learning: Artificial neural networks.

In this section you will learn what deep learning is and how it works, you will also understand what neurons are and how neural networks are trained. We will analyze everything in detail processes associated with neural networks. You too get acquainted with the keras library, tensorflow framework and the google colab development environment.

10
Deep learning: convolutional neural networks.

This section is dedicated to convolutional neural networks, which are used in computer vision. We will detail Let's look at how artificial intelligence recognizes those or other things, such as cars or apples.

11
Practical lessons.

In these lessons, you will develop your own neural networks that will be able to: predict car prices; forecast the output power of power plants (virtual sensor case); predict pump failures based on sensor data; recognize handwritten digits; identify clothing items; recognize various objects; detect damaged vehicles; and recognize street signs for self-driving cars.
